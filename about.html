Our Story

Established in February 2014, Coray General Trading has grown to become a trusted name in building maintenance, renovations, supplying, fencing, gardening, and landscaping services. Based in Kuruman, we operate throughout the region, bringing quality construction solutions to our clients.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide exceptional construction services with integrity, quality, and reliability. Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence through our commitment to delivering projects on time and within budget while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship.
